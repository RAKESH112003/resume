## 📈 Salary Negotiation Tips

1. **Research Your Market Value**
   - [Levels.fyi](https://www.levels.fyi/) - Tech Salaries by Level
   - [PayScale](https://www.payscale.com/)
   - [Blind Salary Database](https://www.teamblind.com/)

2. **Negotiation Strategy**
   - Get offer in writing first
   - Research company & role thoroughly
   - Focus on value, not just salary
   - Include equity/benefits in negotiation

3. **Total Compensation**
   - Base Salary
   - Bonus (typically 10-30% for analyst roles)
   - Stock/Equity
   - Benefits (Health, 401k, PTO)
   - Remote/Flexibility benefits
